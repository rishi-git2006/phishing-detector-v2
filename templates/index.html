How it works

1

Text vectorisation

A TF-IDF vectoriser converts the email body into 8,000 weighted 1–3-word n-gram features, capturing language patterns unique to phishing.

2

Linguistic signals

18 hand-crafted features count urgency phrases, ALL CAPS, credential requests, generic greetings, threats, money baits, and spoofing phrases.

3

URL analysis

Every link is scored across 9 dimensions: IP-based host, URL shorteners, abusive TLDs (.tk/.xyz), brand typosquats, deceptive paths, and more.

4

Ensemble verdict

Naive Bayes, Logistic Regression, and Random Forest are all trained. The best model by macro F1 is selected and served.
